# TechCorp Solutions - Dress Code Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-POL-003

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions maintains a business casual dress code that balances professionalism with comfort. We believe that appropriate attire contributes to a positive work environment while allowing individual expression.

## 2. Business Casual Guidelines

### 2.1 Acceptable Attire

**Tops:**
- Collared shirts, polo shirts, blouses
- Sweaters, cardigans, blazers
- Company-branded apparel
- Clean, wrinkle-free t-shirts (solid colors or subtle patterns)

**Bottoms:**
- Dress pants, chinos, khakis
- Skirts and dresses (knee-length or longer)
- Dark jeans (no rips, tears, or excessive fading)

**Footwear:**
- Dress shoes, loafers, flats
- Clean sneakers (no athletic shoes)
- Boots (ankle or knee-length)
- Sandals (closed-toe preferred)

### 2.2 Not Acceptable

- Athletic wear (gym shorts, yoga pants, sweatpants)
- Flip-flops or beach sandals
- Clothing with offensive graphics or language
- Excessively revealing clothing
- Torn or damaged clothing
- Pajamas or sleepwear

## 3. Client-Facing Days

When meeting with clients or external partners:

- Business professional attire required
- Men: Dress shirt, slacks, blazer optional
- Women: Blouse/dress shirt, dress pants or skirt, blazer optional
- Advance notice provided for client visits

## 4. Casual Fridays

- More relaxed dress code on Fridays
- Jeans (any color) permitted
- Casual t-shirts allowed
- Still no athletic wear or inappropriate attire

## 5. Remote Work Attire

### 5.1 Video Calls

- Business casual from waist up visible on camera
- Professional appearance for client/external meetings
- Background should be tidy and professional

### 5.2 Internal Calls

- Casual attire acceptable
- Camera-on encouraged but not required for all meetings

## 6. Special Circumstances

### 6.1 Theme Days

- Occasional theme days announced by HR (sports jersey day, holiday sweaters)
- Participation voluntary

### 6.2 Weather Considerations

- Extreme weather may warrant relaxed dress code
- HR will communicate any temporary changes

## 7. Personal Grooming

- Maintain neat, clean appearance
- Good personal hygiene expected
- Tattoos and piercings generally acceptable
- Fragrances should be subtle (consideration for allergies)

## 8. Religious and Cultural Attire

TechCorp Solutions respects and accommodates religious and cultural dress requirements:
- Head coverings (hijab, turban, kippah)
- Religious jewelry and symbols
- Cultural attire

Contact HR for any accommodation needs.

## 9. Questions and Concerns

If unsure about appropriate attire:
- Consult your manager
- Contact HR at dress-code@techcorp-solutions.com
- When in doubt, dress more formally

## 10. Policy Violations

- First occurrence: Verbal reminder
- Second occurrence: Written warning
- Repeated violations: May result in being sent home to change (unpaid time)
