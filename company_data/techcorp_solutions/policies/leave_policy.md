# TechCorp Solutions - Leave Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-POL-001

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions is committed to supporting work-life balance for all employees. This policy outlines the various types of leave available and the procedures for requesting time off.

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO)

### 2.1 Annual Vacation Days

| Employment Status | Annual Vacation Days |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Full-time (0-2 years) | 20 days |
| Full-time (3-5 years) | 25 days |
| Full-time (6+ years) | 30 days |
| Part-time | Pro-rated based on hours |

- Vacation days accrue monthly (1/12 of annual entitlement per month)
- Maximum carryover: 5 days to the following year
- Unused days exceeding carryover limit are forfeited on December 31st

### 2.2 How to Request Vacation

1. Submit request through the HR Portal at least **2 weeks in advance**
2. Obtain manager approval
3. For requests longer than 5 consecutive days, submit **4 weeks in advance**
4. Blackout periods may apply during critical business periods (Q4 closing, product launches)

## 3. Sick Leave

### 3.1 Sick Leave Entitlement

- **Full-time employees:** 10 paid sick days per year
- **Part-time employees:** Pro-rated based on work schedule
- Sick days do not carry over to the next year
- Unused sick days are not paid out upon termination

### 3.2 Sick Leave Procedures

- Notify your manager by **9:00 AM** on the day of absence
- For absences of **3 or more consecutive days**, a medical certificate is required
- Extended illness (more than 5 days) may qualify for Short-Term Disability

## 4. Parental Leave

### 4.1 Maternity Leave

- **12 weeks** of fully paid maternity leave
- Additional **4 weeks** at 60% pay (optional)
- Available to employees with at least 12 months of service
- Must provide notice at least 30 days before expected leave start

### 4.2 Paternity Leave

- **4 weeks** of fully paid paternity leave
- Must be taken within 6 months of child's birth or adoption
- Available to employees with at least 6 months of service

### 4.3 Adoption Leave

- Same benefits as biological parents
- Leave begins on the date of placement or legal adoption

## 5. Bereavement Leave

| Relationship | Paid Days |
|--------------|-----------|
| Spouse, Child, Parent | 5 days |
| Sibling, Grandparent | 3 days |
| Extended Family | 1 day |

Additional unpaid leave may be granted upon request.

## 6. Jury Duty

- Full pay for up to **10 days** of jury duty per year
- Provide jury summons to HR within 48 hours of receipt
- Return to work on any day released before 2:00 PM

## 7. Personal Days

- **3 personal days** per year for full-time employees
- Can be used for any reason without explanation
- Request at least 24 hours in advance when possible
- Do not carry over to the next year

## 8. Holidays

TechCorp Solutions observes the following **11 paid holidays**:

1. New Year's Day (January 1)
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day
3. Presidents' Day
4. Memorial Day
5. Independence Day (July 4)
6. Labor Day
7. Thanksgiving Day
8. Day after Thanksgiving
9. Christmas Eve (December 24)
10. Christmas Day (December 25)
11. New Year's Eve (December 31)

### 8.1 Floating Holidays

- **2 floating holidays** per year
- Use for religious observances or personal celebrations
- Request at least 1 week in advance

## 9. Unpaid Leave

### 9.1 Leave of Absence

- Unpaid leave may be granted for up to 30 days
- Requires VP-level approval
- Benefits continue during approved unpaid leave (employee pays full premium)

### 9.2 FMLA Leave

Eligible employees may take up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave under FMLA for:
- Birth and care of a newborn
- Placement of a child for adoption or foster care
- Care of an immediate family member with a serious health condition
- Medical leave when unable to work due to a serious health condition
