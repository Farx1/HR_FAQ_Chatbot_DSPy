# TechCorp Solutions - Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-POL-002

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions embraces flexible work arrangements to enhance productivity and work-life balance. This policy establishes guidelines for remote work (work from home) arrangements.

## 2. Eligibility

### 2.1 Eligible Positions

Remote work is available to employees whose job responsibilities can be performed effectively outside the office. Eligibility is determined by:

- Job function and responsibilities
- Performance history (minimum "Meets Expectations" rating)
- Completion of probationary period (3 months)
- Manager approval

### 2.2 Positions Typically Eligible

- Software Engineers
- Product Managers
- Data Scientists
- Marketing Specialists
- Finance Analysts
- HR Business Partners

### 2.3 Positions Requiring On-Site Presence

- Facilities Management
- IT Hardware Support
- Reception/Front Desk
- Physical Security

## 3. Remote Work Options

### 3.1 Hybrid Remote (Standard)

- Work remotely up to **3 days per week**
- Minimum **2 days in office** required
- Core in-office days may be designated by department
- Engineering: Tuesday, Thursday in-office
- Product: Monday, Wednesday in-office
- Other departments: Flexible with manager approval

### 3.2 Full Remote (Exception-Based)

- Available for specific roles or circumstances
- Requires Director-level approval
- Quarterly in-person meetings may be required
- Travel to headquarters for key events (all-hands, team offsites)

## 4. Equipment and Setup

### 4.1 Company-Provided Equipment

TechCorp Solutions provides the following for remote work:

| Equipment | Provided | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Laptop | Yes | Standard issue MacBook Pro or Dell XPS |
| Monitor | Yes | One 27" monitor for home office |
| Keyboard & Mouse | Yes | Upon request |
| Headset | Yes | Noise-canceling headset for calls |
| Webcam | Yes | HD webcam if laptop camera insufficient |

### 4.2 Home Office Stipend

- **$500 one-time stipend** for home office setup (new hires)
- **$100 annual stipend** for office supplies and maintenance
- Submit receipts through expense system for reimbursement

### 4.3 Internet Requirements

- Minimum **50 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload** speed required
- **$75/month internet allowance** for hybrid remote employees
- **$100/month internet allowance** for full remote employees

## 5. Work Hours and Availability

### 5.1 Core Hours

All employees must be available during **core hours**:
- **10:00 AM - 3:00 PM** (local time)
- Exceptions require manager approval

### 5.2 Flexibility

- Flexible start/end times outside core hours
- Must complete **40 hours per week** (full-time)
- Overtime requires pre-approval

### 5.3 Communication Expectations

- Respond to Slack messages within **2 hours** during work hours
- Email response within **24 hours** (business days)
- Camera on for video meetings (exceptions for bandwidth issues)
- Update calendar with working hours and availability

## 6. Workspace Requirements

### 6.1 Dedicated Workspace

Remote employees must maintain:
- Quiet, private workspace for confidential calls
- Ergonomic setup (proper desk, chair, monitor height)
- Adequate lighting for video calls
- Professional background for video meetings

### 6.2 Security Requirements

- Use company VPN for all work activities
- Lock computer when stepping away
- No work on public WiFi without VPN
- Secure storage for any physical documents
- Report any security concerns immediately

## 7. Performance and Accountability

### 7.1 Performance Standards

Remote work does not change performance expectations:
- Same deliverables and deadlines apply
- Regular 1:1 meetings with manager (weekly recommended)
- Participation in team meetings and standups
- Proactive communication about blockers

### 7.2 Trial Period

New remote arrangements include a **90-day trial period**:
- Manager and employee assess effectiveness
- Adjustments made as needed
- Remote privileges may be modified or revoked if performance declines

## 8. Tax and Legal Considerations

### 8.1 Work Location

- Employees must work from their registered home address
- Working from another state/country requires HR approval
- Tax implications may apply for extended work in different jurisdictions

### 8.2 International Remote Work

- Maximum **4 weeks per year** working from another country
- Requires 30-day advance approval from HR
- Employee responsible for visa/work permit compliance

## 9. Requesting Remote Work

### 9.1 Process

1. Discuss with your manager
2. Submit Remote Work Agreement form via HR Portal
3. Manager reviews and approves/denies within 5 business days
4. HR confirms equipment and stipend eligibility
5. Arrangement begins on agreed start date

### 9.2 Changes to Arrangement

- Request changes at least 2 weeks in advance
- Temporary changes (illness, family emergency) communicated to manager directly
