# TechCorp Solutions - New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-ONB-001

## 1. Welcome to TechCorp Solutions!

Congratulations on joining our team! This checklist will guide you through your first days and weeks at TechCorp Solutions.

## 2. Before Your First Day

### 2.1 Documents to Complete

HR will send these electronically via DocuSign:
- [ ] Employment agreement
- [ ] Tax forms (W-4, state withholding)
- [ ] I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification
- [ ] Direct deposit authorization
- [ ] Emergency contact information
- [ ] Confidentiality agreement
- [ ] Invention assignment agreement
- [ ] Employee handbook acknowledgment

### 2.2 What to Bring on Day 1

- Government-issued photo ID (for I-9 verification)
- Social Security card or passport
- Voided check (for direct deposit)
- Personal laptop charger (if applicable)

## 4. First Week

### 4.1 Required Training (Complete by Day 5)

- [ ] Security Awareness Training (2 hours)
- [ ] Anti-Harassment Training (1 hour)
- [ ] Code of Conduct Review (30 min)
- [ ] Data Privacy Training (1 hour)
- [ ] Company Overview (1 hour)

### 4.2 Meetings to Schedule

- [ ] 1:1 with your manager (daily during first week)
- [ ] Meet your team members
- [ ] Meet your onboarding buddy
- [ ] Meet key stakeholders
- [ ] Department overview with team lead

### 4.3 Administrative Tasks

- [ ] Set up voicemail
- [ ] Update Slack profile (photo, title, pronouns)
- [ ] Review and update HR Portal profile
- [ ] Enroll in benefits (within 30 days)
- [ ] Set up 401(k) (optional, auto-enrolled at 3%)
- [ ] Join relevant Slack channels

## 5. First Month (30 Days)

### 5.1 Goals

- [ ] Complete all required training
- [ ] Understand team goals and your role
- [ ] Complete first small project or task
- [ ] Have 30-day check-in with manager
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding experience

### 5.2 Training to Complete

- [ ] Role-specific training modules
- [ ] Product/service overview
- [ ] Tools and systems deep-dive
- [ ] Department-specific compliance

### 5.3 Meetings

- [ ] Weekly 1:1 with manager
- [ ] Team meetings
- [ ] All-hands meeting (monthly)
- [ ] Cross-functional introductions

## 6. First 90 Days (Probation Period)

### 6.1 Milestones

**Day 30:**
- Complete all onboarding training
- Understand role expectations
- Begin contributing to team projects

**Day 60:**
- Independently handle routine tasks
- Build relationships across team
- Identify areas for growth

**Day 90:**
- Full productivity expected
- Probation review with manager
- Set goals for next quarter

### 6.2 Probation Review

At 90 days, you'll have a formal review covering:
- Performance against expectations
- Cultural fit
- Areas of strength
- Development opportunities
- Confirmation of continued employment

## 9. Tips for Success

### 9.1 Do's

- Ask questions - no question is too small
- Take notes during meetings
- Reach out to your buddy for guidance
- Be proactive about meeting colleagues
- Provide feedback on your onboarding experience

### 9.2 Don'ts

- Don't be afraid to ask for help
- Don't skip required training
- Don't miss your benefits enrollment deadline (30 days)
- Don't hesitate to speak up if something isn't working
