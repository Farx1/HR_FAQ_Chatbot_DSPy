# TechCorp Solutions - Training & Development Program

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-ONB-003

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions is committed to employee development through comprehensive training programs. This document outlines mandatory training, optional development opportunities, and career growth resources.

## 2. Mandatory Training

| Training | Duration | Deadline |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Company Orientation | 2 hours | Day 1 |
| Security Awareness | 2 hours | Day 5 |
| Anti-Harassment & Discrimination | 1 hour | Day 5 |

## 7. Mentorship Program

### 7.1 Program Structure

- 6-month formal mentorship cycles
- Matching based on goals and expertise
- Monthly mentor-mentee meetings (minimum)
- Mid-point and final check-ins with HR

### 7.2 Mentor Expectations

- Meet at least monthly
- Provide guidance and feedback
- Share experiences and network
- Support mentee's development goals

### 7.3 Mentee Expectations

- Drive the relationship
- Come prepared to meetings
- Act on feedback and advice
- Complete program surveys

### 7.4 How to Participate

- Sign up via HR Portal
- Complete profile with goals
- Matching occurs quarterly
- Kickoff session with HR
