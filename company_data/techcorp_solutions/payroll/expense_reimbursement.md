# TechCorp Solutions - Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-PAY-003

## 1. Overview

This policy establishes guidelines for business expense reimbursement at TechCorp Solutions. Employees are expected to exercise good judgment and spend company money as if it were their own.

## 2. General Principles

### 2.1 Expense Guidelines

- Expenses must be **business-related** and **reasonable**
- Pre-approval required for expenses over **$500**
- Receipts required for all expenses over **$25**
- Submit expenses within **30 days** of incurring them

### 2.2 Corporate Card

- Corporate Amex issued to employees with regular travel
- Personal charges on corporate card are prohibited
- Lost/stolen cards: Report immediately to Finance

## 3. Travel Expenses

### 3.1 Air Travel

| Class | Policy |
|-------|--------|
| Domestic (under 4 hours) | Economy class |
| Domestic (4+ hours) | Economy Plus/Comfort+ |
| International (under 6 hours) | Economy Plus |
| International (6+ hours) | Business class (VP+ approval) |

**Booking Requirements:**
- Book through Concur or approved travel agent
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Choose refundable fares for uncertain travel

### 3.2 Lodging

| Location Type | Nightly Limit |
|---------------|---------------|
| Major cities (NYC, SF, London) | $350 |
| Other US cities | $250 |
| International | Market rate (pre-approved) |

**Guidelines:**
- Book standard rooms at business-class hotels
- Marriott, Hilton, Hyatt preferred (corporate rates)
- Extended stays (5+ nights): Consider Airbnb with approval

### 3.3 Ground Transportation

| Type | Policy |
|------|--------|
| Rental car | Compact/midsize; full coverage insurance |
| Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) | Standard service; no luxury |
| Taxi | When rideshare unavailable |
| Public transit | Always encouraged |
| Personal vehicle | $0.67/mile (IRS rate 2024) |

### 3.4 Meals During Travel

| Meal | Per Diem Limit |
|------|----------------|
| Breakfast | $20 |
| Lunch | $30 |
| Dinner | $50 |
| Daily Total | $100 |

**Notes:**
- Alcohol reimbursed only with client meals (reasonable amounts)
- Tips included in limits (15-20% standard)
- Per diem covers all food; no additional snack claims

## 4. Client Entertainment

### 4.1 Guidelines

- Must have clear business purpose
- Document attendees and business discussed
- Manager approval required for expenses over $200

### 4.2 Limits

| Event Type | Per Person Limit |
|------------|------------------|
| Business meal | $100 |
| Team dinner | $75 |
| Client event | $150 |
| Sporting event/show | $200 (requires Director approval) |

### 4.3 Prohibited

- Entertainment without business purpose
- Personal celebrations charged to company
- Expenses for family members (unless pre-approved)

## 5. Office & Equipment

### 5.1 Home Office

One-time setup (new hires):
- $500 stipend for desk, chair, accessories
- Monitor provided by IT

Ongoing:
- $100/year for supplies
- $75-100/month internet allowance

### 5.2 Software & Subscriptions

- Pre-approval required for any software purchase
- Use approved tools from IT catalog when possible
- Annual subscriptions preferred over monthly

### 5.3 Office Supplies

- Order through approved vendors (Staples, Amazon Business)
- Manager approval for orders over $100
- No personal items

## 6. Professional Development

### 6.1 Covered Expenses

- Conference registration (up to $3,000)
- Course fees (within tuition benefit limits)
- Books and publications (up to $500/year)
- Professional memberships (up to $500/year)

### 6.2 Approval

- Expenses under $500: Manager approval
- Expenses $500-$2,000: Director approval
- Expenses over $2,000: VP approval

## 7. Team Events & Morale

### 7.1 Team Events

- $100/person/quarter for team activities
- Manager plans and approves
- Must be inclusive (consider dietary restrictions, accessibility)

### 7.2 Examples

- Team lunches/dinners
- Escape rooms, bowling, activities
- Virtual team events (food delivery for remote)

### 7.3 Not Covered

- Alcohol-only events
- Activities excluding team members
- Personal celebrations (birthdays, etc.)

## 8. Expense Submission

### 8.1 Process

1. Log into Concur expense system
2. Create expense report
3. Attach receipts (photo or PDF)
4. Add business justification
5. Submit for approval

### 8.2 Required Information

- Date of expense
- Vendor/merchant name
- Amount and currency
- Business purpose
- Attendees (for meals/entertainment)

### 8.3 Receipt Requirements

| Amount | Receipt Required |
|--------|------------------|
| Under $25 | No (but recommended) |
| $25-$75 | Yes (photo acceptable) |
| Over $75 | Yes (itemized receipt) |

### 8.4 Missing Receipts

- Complete Missing Receipt Affidavit
- Manager approval required
- Pattern of missing receipts may result in denied expenses

## 9. Approval Workflow

### 9.1 Standard Approval

| Amount | Approver |
|--------|----------|
| Under $500 | Direct manager |
| $500-$2,500 | Director |
| $2,500-$10,000 | VP |
| Over $10,000 | CFO |

### 9.2 Timeline

- Manager approval: Within 5 business days
- Finance processing: Within 5 business days of approval
- Reimbursement: Next payroll cycle

## 10. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are **not reimbursable**:

- Personal expenses
- Traffic/parking violations
- Airline club memberships (unless pre-approved)
- Travel insurance (covered by corporate policy)
- Laundry (unless travel exceeds 5 days)
- In-room movies or entertainment
- Mini-bar charges
- Gym fees (covered by wellness benefit)
- Childcare
- Pet care
- Personal phone charges

## 11. International Travel

### 11.1 Additional Considerations

- Obtain travel approval 2 weeks in advance
- Check visa requirements
- Register trip with corporate security
- Carry emergency contact card

### 11.2 Currency

- Expenses in foreign currency converted at transaction rate
- Corporate card provides best exchange rates
- Keep receipts in original currency

## 12. Violations

Expense policy violations may result in:
- Denied reimbursement
- Required repayment
- Disciplinary action
- Termination (for fraud)
