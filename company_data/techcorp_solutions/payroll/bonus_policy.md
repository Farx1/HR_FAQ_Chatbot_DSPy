# TechCorp Solutions - Bonus & Incentive Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-PAY-002

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions rewards exceptional performance through various bonus and incentive programs. This policy outlines the types of bonuses available, eligibility criteria, and payout processes.

## 2. Annual Performance Bonus

### 2.1 Eligibility

- All full-time employees
- Must be employed by October 1 of the bonus year
- Must be employed at time of payout (typically February)
- Pro-rated for employees hired after January 1

### 2.2 Target Bonus Percentages

| Level | Target Bonus (% of Base) |
|-------|--------------------------|
| Individual Contributor (E1-E3, P1-P3) | 10% |
| Senior IC (E4-E6, P4-P5) | 15% |
| Manager (M1-M2) | 15% |
| Director (M3-M4) | 20% |
| VP and above (M5+) | 25-40% |

### 2.3 Bonus Calculation

**Formula:** Base Salary × Target % × Company Multiplier × Individual Multiplier

**Company Multiplier (0-150%):**
Based on company performance against annual goals:
- Revenue targets
- Profitability
- Strategic objectives

**Individual Multiplier (0-150%):**
Based on individual performance rating:
| Rating | Multiplier |
|--------|------------|
| Exceptional | 125-150% |
| Exceeds Expectations | 110-125% |
| Meets Expectations | 90-110% |
| Developing | 50-90% |
| Does Not Meet | 0% |

### 2.4 Example Calculation

- Base Salary: $150,000
- Target Bonus: 15%
- Company Multiplier: 110%
- Individual Multiplier: 120%

**Bonus = $150,000 × 15% × 110% × 120% = $29,700**

### 2.5 Payout Timeline

- Performance year: January 1 - December 31
- Bonus finalized: Late January
- Payout: February paycheck

## 3. Signing Bonus

### 3.1 Eligibility

- New hires in competitive roles
- Determined during offer negotiation
- Not guaranteed for all positions

### 3.2 Typical Amounts

| Level | Typical Range |
|-------|---------------|
| Entry Level | $5,000 - $10,000 |
| Mid Level | $10,000 - $25,000 |
| Senior Level | $25,000 - $50,000 |
| Executive | $50,000+ |

### 3.3 Repayment Terms

| Departure Timing | Repayment |
|------------------|-----------|
| Within 6 months | 100% |
| 6-12 months | 50% |
| After 12 months | $0 |

## 4. Referral Bonus

### 4.1 Program Overview

Employees receive bonuses for successful referrals who are hired and complete probation.

### 4.2 Bonus Amounts

| Role Type | Bonus Amount |
|-----------|--------------|
| Engineering (E3+) | $10,000 |
| Engineering (E1-E2) | $5,000 |
| Product/Design | $7,500 |
| Sales | $5,000 |
| Other roles | $3,000 |
| Executive | $15,000 |

### 4.3 Eligibility Rules

- Referrer must be current employee at time of hire and payout
- Referred candidate cannot have applied in past 6 months
- HR, recruiting, and hiring managers not eligible for referrals to their team
- Bonus paid 50% at hire, 50% after 90 days

### 4.4 How to Refer

1. Submit referral through HR Portal
2. Include candidate's resume and your recommendation
3. Recruiting team reviews and reaches out
4. Track referral status in portal

## 5. Spot Bonuses

### 5.1 Purpose

Immediate recognition for exceptional contributions:
- Major project completion
- Going above and beyond
- Critical problem solving
- Customer success wins

### 5.2 Amounts

| Recognition Level | Amount |
|-------------------|--------|
| Bronze | $250 |
| Silver | $500 |
| Gold | $1,000 |
| Platinum | $2,500 |

### 5.3 Nomination Process

1. Any employee can nominate a colleague
2. Manager approval required
3. HR processes within 2 pay periods
4. Recipient notified with recognition message

### 5.4 Budget

- Each manager has quarterly spot bonus budget
- Unused budget does not roll over
- Additional budget available for exceptional circumstances

## 6. Retention Bonus

### 6.1 When Offered

- Critical talent at risk of departure
- Key employees during acquisitions/transitions
- Specialized skills in high demand

### 6.2 Structure

- Typically 10-25% of annual salary
- Paid in installments over 12-24 months
- Requires signed retention agreement
- Repayment required if voluntary departure before term

## 7. Sales Incentives

### 7.1 Commission Structure

Sales roles have separate commission plans:
- On-Target Earnings (OTE) = Base + Commission
- Commission typically 40-60% of OTE
- Accelerators for exceeding quota

### 7.2 Sales Bonus Programs

- President's Club (top performers)
- Quarterly SPIFs (Sales Performance Incentive Funds)
- New logo bonuses
- Renewal bonuses

Contact Sales Operations for detailed commission plans.

## 9. Tax Implications

### 9.1 Withholding

Bonuses are subject to:
- Federal supplemental income tax (22% or 37% for >$1M)
- State income tax
- Social Security and Medicare

### 9.2 Timing

- Bonuses taxed in the year paid, not earned
- Consider tax planning for large bonuses
