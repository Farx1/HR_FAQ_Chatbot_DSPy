# TechCorp Solutions - Salary & Compensation Structure

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-PAY-001

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions is committed to fair, competitive, and transparent compensation. Our salary structure is designed to attract and retain top talent while ensuring internal equity.

## 2. Compensation Philosophy

### 2.1 Guiding Principles

- **Market Competitive:** Target 75th percentile of market rates
- **Pay for Performance:** Reward high performers
- **Internal Equity:** Fair pay across similar roles
- **Transparency:** Clear salary bands and progression

### 2.2 Total Compensation Components

1. Base Salary
2. Annual Bonus
3. Equity (Stock Options/RSUs)
4. Benefits
5. Perks & Allowances

## 4. Geographic Adjustments

### 4.1 Location Tiers

| Tier | Locations | Adjustment |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Tier 1 | San Francisco, New York, Seattle | 100% (baseline) |
| Tier 2 | Los Angeles, Boston, Washington DC | 95% |
| Tier 3 | Austin, Denver, Chicago, Portland | 90% |
| Tier 4 | Other US locations | 85% |
| International | Varies by country | Market-based |

### 4.2 Remote Work Adjustments

- Salary based on employee's registered home location
- Relocation may trigger salary adjustment
- 6-month notice required for location changes

## 5. Pay Periods & Methods

### 5.1 Pay Schedule

- **Pay Frequency:** Bi-weekly (every other Friday)
- **Pay Periods:** 26 per year
- **Direct Deposit:** Required for all employees

### 5.2 Payroll Calendar 2024

Pay dates fall on alternate Fridays. If payday falls on a holiday, payment is made the preceding business day.

### 5.3 Paycheck Components

Each paycheck includes:
- Gross pay (base salary รท 26)
- Bonus payments (when applicable)
- Deductions (taxes, benefits, 401k)
- Net pay

## 6. Salary Reviews

### 6.1 Annual Review Cycle

| Month | Activity |
|-------|----------|
| October | Performance reviews begin |
| November | Calibration sessions |
| December | Compensation decisions finalized |
| January | New salaries effective |
| February | Salary letters distributed |

### 6.2 Merit Increase Guidelines

| Performance Rating | Typical Increase |
|--------------------|------------------|
| Exceeds Expectations | 5-8% |
| Meets Expectations | 3-5% |
| Developing | 0-2% |
| Does Not Meet | 0% |

### 6.3 Promotion Increases

- Typical promotion increase: **10-15%**
- Must move to new level's salary band
- Effective on promotion date

## 7. Requesting a Salary Review

### 7.1 Off-Cycle Reviews

Off-cycle salary reviews may be requested for:
- Significant change in responsibilities
- Market adjustment (with data)
- Retention concerns
- Equity issues

### 7.2 Process

1. Discuss with manager
2. Manager submits request to HR
3. HR reviews market data and internal equity
4. Decision communicated within 30 days
