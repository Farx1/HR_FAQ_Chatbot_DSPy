# TechCorp Solutions - Health Insurance Benefits

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-BEN-001

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions provides comprehensive health insurance coverage to all eligible employees and their dependents. We partner with BlueCross BlueShield to offer multiple plan options to meet diverse needs.

## 2. Eligibility

### 2.1 Employee Eligibility

- Full-time employees (30+ hours/week): Eligible from first day of employment
- Part-time employees (20-29 hours/week): Eligible after 90 days
- Coverage begins on the first of the month following hire date

### 2.2 Dependent Eligibility

Eligible dependents include:
- Legal spouse or domestic partner
- Children under age 26 (biological, adopted, step-children)
- Disabled adult children (with documentation)

## 4. Premium Costs

### 4.1 Monthly Employee Contributions

| Plan | Employee Only | Employee + Spouse | Employee + Child(ren) | Family |
|------|---------------|-------------------|----------------------|--------|
| PPO | $150 | $350 | $300 | $500 |
| HDHP | $75 | $200 | $175 | $300 |

**TechCorp pays approximately 80% of total premium costs.**

### 4.2 Premium Deductions

- Deducted from paycheck bi-weekly (24 pay periods)
- Pre-tax deductions reduce taxable income

## 5. Dental Coverage

| Service | Coverage |

## 6. Vision Coverage

Vision insurance is included with all health plans at no additional cost.

| Service | Coverage |

| Eye Exam | $10 copay (once per year) |
| Frames | $150 allowance (once per year) |
| Lenses | Covered in full (once per year) |

## 7. Mental Health Coverage

Mental health services are covered at parity with medical services:

- Outpatient therapy: Same copay as specialist visit
- Inpatient mental health: Same as hospital stay
- Substance abuse treatment: Covered
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 8 free sessions per year

## 8. Enrollment

### 8.1 Initial Enrollment

- Enroll within **30 days** of hire date
- Coverage begins first of month following enrollment
- If not enrolled within 30 days, must wait until Open Enrollment

### 8.2 Open Enrollment

- Annual open enrollment period: **November 1-15**
- Changes effective January 1
- Review and update beneficiaries annually

### 8.3 Qualifying Life Events

Changes allowed within **30 days** of:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status

## 9. COBRA Continuation

If you leave TechCorp, you may continue coverage through COBRA:
- Coverage for up to 18 months (36 months for certain events)
- You pay full premium plus 2% administrative fee
- Election must be made within 60 days of qualifying event
