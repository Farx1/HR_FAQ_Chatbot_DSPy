# TechCorp Solutions - Wellness Program

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-BEN-003

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions is committed to supporting the physical, mental, and financial well-being of our employees. Our comprehensive wellness program offers resources and incentives to help you live a healthier life.

## 2. Eligibility

- All full-time and part-time employees
- Benefits begin on first day of employment
- Some programs extend to dependents

## 3. Fitness Benefits

### 3.1 Gym Membership Subsidy

- **$100/month** reimbursement for gym membership
- Covers any gym, fitness studio, or online fitness program
- Submit receipts monthly through expense system

### 3.2 On-Site Fitness Center (HQ Only)

San Francisco headquarters offers:
- Full gym with cardio and weight equipment
- Yoga and meditation room
- Locker rooms with showers
- Open 24/7 with badge access

### 3.3 Fitness Classes

- Weekly virtual fitness classes (yoga, HIIT, stretching)
- Free for all employees
- Schedule on wellness portal

### 3.4 Step Challenge

- Quarterly company-wide step challenges
- Prizes for top performers and most improved
- Sync Fitbit, Apple Watch, or other trackers

## 4. Mental Health Support

### 4.1 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

**8 free counseling sessions per year** covering:
- Stress and anxiety
- Depression
- Relationship issues
- Work-life balance
- Grief and loss
- Substance abuse

**Access:** 1-800-555-HELP (24/7 confidential hotline)

### 4.2 Mental Health Days

- **2 mental health days** per year (in addition to sick leave)
- No documentation required
- Notify manager same-day

### 4.3 Meditation & Mindfulness

- Free Headspace subscription for all employees
- Weekly guided meditation sessions (virtual)
- Quiet rooms available at all offices

### 4.4 Mental Health Training

- Annual mental health awareness training
- Manager training on supporting team mental health
- Resources for recognizing signs of distress

## 5. Preventive Care

### 5.1 Annual Health Screening

Free annual health screening including:
- Biometric screening (blood pressure, cholesterol, glucose)
- Health risk assessment
- Personalized health report
- Complete screening to earn wellness incentive

### 5.2 Flu Shots

- Free flu shots at all office locations (fall)
- Reimbursement for flu shots obtained elsewhere

### 5.3 Preventive Care Coverage

All preventive care is covered at 100% under health plans:
- Annual physical exam
- Immunizations
- Cancer screenings
- Well-woman visits

## 6. Nutrition Support

### 6.1 Healthy Snacks

- Free healthy snacks in all office kitchens
- Fresh fruit delivered weekly
- Filtered water stations

### 6.2 Nutrition Counseling

- 4 free sessions with registered dietitian per year
- Virtual appointments available
- Personalized meal planning

### 6.3 Meal Subsidies

- $15/day meal subsidy when working in office
- Healthy options highlighted in cafeteria
- Special dietary needs accommodated

## 7. Financial Wellness

### 7.1 Financial Planning

- Free annual session with financial advisor
- Retirement planning assistance
- Student loan counseling

### 7.2 Student Loan Assistance

- **$100/month** student loan repayment assistance
- Available after 1 year of employment
- Maximum $1,200/year

### 7.3 Emergency Savings Program

- Payroll deduction to emergency savings account
- TechCorp matches first $500 saved (one-time)

### 7.4 Financial Education

- Monthly financial wellness webinars
- Topics: budgeting, investing, home buying, retirement
- On-demand financial education library

## 8. Work-Life Balance

### 8.1 Flexible Work Arrangements

- Hybrid remote work (3 days remote/week)
- Flexible start/end times
- Compressed work week options (with manager approval)

### 8.2 Sabbatical Program

After **7 years** of service:
- 4-week paid sabbatical
- Additional 4 weeks unpaid (optional)
- Can be used for travel, education, volunteer work

### 8.3 Volunteer Time Off

- **16 hours** paid volunteer time per year
- Use for any charitable organization
- Group volunteer events organized quarterly

## 9. Family Support

### 9.1 Childcare Support

- **$5,000/year** dependent care FSA (pre-tax)
- Backup childcare: 10 days/year subsidized
- Lactation rooms at all offices

### 9.2 Elder Care Support

- Elder care resources through EAP
- Backup elder care: 5 days/year subsidized
- Caregiver support groups

### 9.3 Fertility Benefits

- $15,000 lifetime fertility treatment benefit
- Covers IVF, egg freezing, fertility medications
- Adoption assistance: $5,000 per adoption

## 10. Wellness Incentives

### 10.1 Annual Wellness Incentive

Earn up to **$500/year** in wellness rewards:

| Activity | Reward |
|----------|--------|
| Complete health screening | $100 |
| Complete health assessment | $50 |
| Participate in step challenge | $50 |
| Complete wellness workshop | $25/each (max 4) |
| Log 150+ exercise minutes/week | $25/month |

### 10.2 Reward Redemption

- Rewards deposited to HSA, added to paycheck, or donated to charity
- Rewards distributed quarterly
