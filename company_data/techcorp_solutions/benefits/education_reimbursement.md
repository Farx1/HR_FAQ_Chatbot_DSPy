# TechCorp Solutions - Education & Professional Development

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2024  
**Policy Number:** HR-BEN-004

## 1. Overview

TechCorp Solutions invests in employee growth through comprehensive education and professional development benefits. We believe continuous learning benefits both employees and the organization.

## 2. Eligibility

- Full-time employees after **6 months** of employment
- Part-time employees after **12 months** (pro-rated benefits)
- Must maintain "Meets Expectations" or higher performance rating

## 3. Tuition Reimbursement

### 3.1 Annual Benefit

| Education Type | Annual Maximum |
|----------------|----------------|
| Undergraduate degree | $5,250 |
| Graduate degree (MBA, MS) | $10,000 |
| Professional certifications | $3,000 |
| Individual courses | $2,500 |

### 3.2 Eligible Programs

**Approved:**
- Accredited degree programs (related to job or career path)
- Professional certifications (industry-recognized)
- Technical training courses
- Leadership development programs
- Language courses (business-relevant)

**Not Covered:**
- Recreational courses
- Programs not from accredited institutions
- Courses failed or incomplete
- Programs started before approval

### 3.3 Approval Process

1. Submit Education Reimbursement Request via HR Portal
2. Include course description, cost breakdown, relevance to role
3. Manager approval required
4. HR reviews and approves within 10 business days
5. Approval valid for duration of program

### 3.4 Reimbursement Requirements

- Minimum grade of **B** (or "Pass" for pass/fail courses)
- Submit receipts and transcripts within 60 days of course completion
- Reimbursement processed within 30 days of submission

### 3.5 Service Commitment

| Benefit Received | Service Commitment |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Up to $5,000 | 1 year |
| $5,001 - $10,000 | 2 years |
| Over $10,000 | 3 years |

If you leave before commitment period, pro-rated repayment required.

## 4. Professional Certifications

### 4.1 Supported Certifications

**Technology:**
- AWS (Solutions Architect, Developer, etc.)
- Google Cloud Professional
- Microsoft Azure
- Kubernetes (CKA, CKAD)
- Scrum Master (CSM, PSM)

**Project Management:**
- PMP (Project Management Professional)
- PRINCE2
- Agile certifications

**Other:**
- SHRM (HR certifications)

### 4.2 Certification Benefits

- Exam fees covered (up to 2 attempts)

- Certification bonus: $500-$2,000 upon passing

### 4.3 Certification Bonuses

| Certification Level | Bonus |
|--------------------|-------|
| Associate level | $500 |
| Professional level | $1,000 |
| Expert/Specialty level | $2,000 |

## 5. Learning & Development Budget

### 5.1 Annual L&D Budget

Each employee receives **$2,000/year** for professional development:

**Eligible Uses:**
- Conferences and seminars
- Online courses (Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning)
- Books and publications
- Professional memberships
- Workshops and bootcamps

### 5.2 How to Use

- Pre-approval required for expenses over $500
- Submit expenses through regular expense system
- Tag as "Professional Development"

## 7. Conference Attendance

### 7.2 Speaking Opportunities

- Full conference expenses covered

- Extra PTO day for preparation
